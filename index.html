Spider-man:NWH Review

A look into one of the best marvel movies;

'Captivating', 'Spectacular', 'entertaining' are some of the words fans used to describe this movie when it came out. From the acting to the plot to the cinematography, this movie surpassed many people's expectations.

This movie integrated the older spidermans which was partly why a lot of fans enjoyed it so much. The acting was great, the cinematography was beautiful and that is why it is easily one of the best movies of 2021.

To top it all off, the chemistry between Zendaya and Tom Holland is truly unbeatable.

Overall, this was a great movie and I would rate it a 9.5/10
